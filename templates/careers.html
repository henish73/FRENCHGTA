Careers at FRENCH.GTA

Join our team of passionate individuals dedicated to providing exceptional French language education in the Greater Toronto Area. We're always looking for talented and enthusiastic people to join us!

Current Openings

please email us at frenchgta.ca@gmail.com

We are always interested in hearing from qualified French instructors and other professionals who share our passion for language education. If you don't see a specific opening that matches your skills, feel free to send us your resume and cover letter for future consideration.

Why Work at FRENCH.GTA?

  • Be part of a supportive and collaborative team.
  • Make a positive impact on students' lives.
  • Work in a dynamic and rewarding environment.
  • Enjoy opportunities for professional growth and development.
  • Competitive compensation and benefits.

How to Apply

Please send your resume and cover letter to frenchgta.ca@gmail.com. In your cover letter, please indicate the type of position you are interested in and why you believe you would be a good fit for FRENCH.GTA.
